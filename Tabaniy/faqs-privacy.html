Frequently Asked Questions

What is the adoption fee?

+

Adoption fees vary depending on the pet's age, health status, and breed. Typically, adoption fees range from 50 to 200 USD. This fee includes initial vaccinations, health checks, and microchipping.

How long does the adoption process take?

+

The adoption process typically takes 3-7 business days. This includes application review, home verification (if required), and final approval. In urgent cases, we can expedite the process.

Can I return the pet if it doesn't work out?

+

Yes, we have a 2-week trial period during which you can return the pet if there are compatibility issues. After this period, we offer support and guidance to help ensure a successful adoption.

What if I have allergies?

+

We have hypoallergenic and low-shedding breeds available. Please mention your allergies during the application process, and we'll help match you with a suitable pet.

Do you provide post-adoption support?

+

Absolutely! We provide lifetime support including behavioral consultation, veterinary referrals, and ongoing assistance. You can contact us anytime for advice or concerns.

Are there age restrictions for adopters?

+

Adopters must be at least 18 years old. We also require a valid ID and stable living situation to ensure the pet's well-being.
